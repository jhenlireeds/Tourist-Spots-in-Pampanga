Zoocobia Fun Zoo



Zoocobia Fun Zoo offers a fun filled activity for the family and educational attractions. It is an adventure zoo themepark that gives a variety of exciting and heartstopping rides. Inside Zoocobia you will find an array of colorful birds where you can feed and see them up-close and personal as well as other interesting wild animal species.


Product Info


  • Discover a new must-see destination north of Manila
  • Hop on thrilling rides and meet a range of colorful wild animals
  • Enjoy bonding with the entire family in this zoo and theme park for all ages

    • What You Can Expect


    Connect with nature in the heart of a bustling city with an array of family-friendly activities, such as picking fresh mangoes and bottle-feeding baby animals!

    Enjoy close encounters with farm creatures, including goats, sheep, and pot-bellied pigs, at The Barn. Delight over birds of paradise as they chirp melodiously and perch on sky-high branches. Get a different kind of animal experience at the Menagerie where well-preserved stuffed animals are on display.

    Step into the Garden Maze with lush animal topiaries! Adventurous types won't want to miss going deeper into the lush labyrinth and trying to find their way out of the winding maze.

    Visit Zoocobia Fun Zoo to get away from the city and gain a deeper appreciation of the Philippines' animals and plants!
