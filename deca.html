Deca Clark Wakeboard



Deca Clark Wakeboard is a cable wakeboard park situated in Margot, Angeles City, which is behind the perimeter wall of Clark Freeport zone in Pampanga. Opened in December 2012, this action-packed leisure spot is especially designed for both trained and aspiring wakeboard riders. The park features a full-size 5-point Rixen main cable that will pull you across the lake at a constant speed. The main lake has five corners with obstacles like an A-frame, curved kicker, and wedge, in between. Meanwhile, first-time or less experienced wakeboards can learn and practice the basics in the separate Easy Start Beginners' Area.


How to get there

Deca Clark Wakeboard, just a short drive away from north of Manila, is conveniently located in the sprawling Angeles City and is near tourist destinations and shops. You can take the North Luzon Express Way and SCTEX or Subic - Clark - Tarlac Expressway for faster route.


It is also a five to ten minute drive away from Clark International Airport, Duty Free Shops, SM City Clark and can be accessed via SCTEX through Clark interchange or inside Clark by taking the Friendship Hi-Way, also known as Clark side gate or taking the Sapang Bato Gate


Best time to visit


It is best to book a session here in the dry months of December to May as there are fewer chances of tropical rainstorms during this time. However, visitors may still go wakeboarding in light to moderate rain. Lifestyle and aspiring riders can also spend their wakeboarding holidays or trainings in this park as it is open every day from 8 AM to 6 PM.
