Aqua Planet



Aqua Planet is known to be the biggest water park in the Philippines which is located at Clark Freeport and Special Economic Zone in the province of Pampanga. It offers tourists a swimming ride, outdoor food court, stalls, a commemorative shop, elegant shower rooms, grand facilities, and water attractions for every age. Inside the water park, there is a separate waterslide and wave pool which tourists can choose from.

Aqua Planet has a total of thirty-eight slides and attractions which are surrounded by lush vegetation. It is believed to be the most modern water park in the Philippines. It is only fifty minutes away from Subic Bay via Subic-Clark-Tarlac Expressway (SCTEX) and fifteen minutes away from Clark International Airport and SM City Clark. In addition, it is only a two hours and thirty minutes drive from Metro Manila. Aqua Planet has a size of ten hectares or 100,000 square meters that can accommodate five thousand tourists daily.

The story goes like this, once upon a time the affectionate water god name Halia and the courageous fire god name Pinatubo loved each other. As time goes by they were separated by the god of heaven named Zeus. Pinatubo was sent to the mortal world and for this reason, it turned him into a volcano. On the other hand, Halia beseeches Zeus to turn her into a charming water planet so she could be with Pinatubo. The god of heaven accepted her request and the water planet was named Halia Aqua Planet. Presently, people living in Angeles City Pampanga deeply think whenever it rains in their region, the attractive water god Halia misses the courageous fire god Pinatubo.



According to its past history, Aqua Planet was established by Bb International Leisure And Resort Development Corporation which is a Filipino-Taiwanese business firm. The water park cost 2.5 billion pesos and it was planned by Taiwanese engineers and architects. The main attraction was designed by Aquatic Development Group for a brand new state-of-the-art water park in the Philippines with a large size wave pool.




How to Go to Aqua Planet



Option 1


  1. From Makati City, ride an MRT train going to Landmark Trinoma.
  2. From Landmark Trinoma, ride a premium point-to-point bus (P2P) which is operated by Genesis that is bound to Clark International Airport (CRK). Tell the bus driver or bus conductor to drop you off at SM City Clark Pampanga. Afterward, walk for seven minutes going to Bayanihan Terminal.
  3. At the Bayanihan Terminal, ride a passenger jeep that is bound to Aqua Planet.

Option 2

  1. From Cubao Quezon City, ride a Victory Liner regular aircon bus bound to Lingayen Pangasinan. Tell the bus driver to alight you at Dau Bus Terminal in Mabalacat, Pampanga.
  2. From Dau Bus Terminal, ride a tricycle going to Bayanihan Terminal in Angeles Pampanga. Then ride a Grab car that will directly take you in front of Aqua Planet.

By private vehicle:

  1. From your Google Maps or Waze app on your cellphone, pin the Aqua Planet water park for proper direction.
  2. From Manila, take the Metro Manila Skyway then proceed to North Luzon Expressway (NLEX). Once you reach the NLEX Dau Exit in Mabalacat Pampanga, turn left to go to Angeles City and drive until you approach the point where you need to turn right going to Clark Freeport and Special Economic Zone where Aqua Planet is situated.
